Absolutely. We offer optional setup services for all templates. Contact us at hello@elitestudioagency.ca for a custom installation quote.

Due to the digital nature of our products, all sales are final. However, if you experience any issues we will work with you to resolve them promptly.

Single-purchase licenses cover one website. For agency or multi-use licenses, please contact us for pricing on extended licenses.

Absolutely. While our guides reference local Toronto/Scarborough examples, the strategies apply to any local business in any Canadian or North American city.
